## Patterns to Extract (Preview)

These are the cross-season patterns we'll formalize in Phase 5B:

1. Ball games → flywheel intake + shooter (2016, 2017, 2020, 2022, 2024)
2. Object placement games → elevator + arm (2018, 2019, 2023, 2025)
3. Swerve adoption curve: 2017 (novelty) → 2022 (standard) → 2024 (universal)
4. 254's consistent pattern: technical binder + feedforward-dominant control
5. 4414's emergence: simple/rigid/small beats complex/heavy every time
6. 6328's contribution: transparency + logging → community-wide improvement
7. 1323's pattern: when they compete, they win (3 championships in 4 attended years)
