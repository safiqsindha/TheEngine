## How to Read This Document

Each table shows the WINNING ALLIANCE SCORE (combined 3-robot total)
at each competition level. To find YOUR robot's target contribution,
divide by 3 for equal contribution, or by 2.5 if you want to be
the strongest robot on your alliance.

**Your target EPA = Winning Score ÷ 2.5 to 3.0**

## Cross-Game Patterns

### Score inflation across competition levels (normalized)

| Level | Relative to District Qual Winner | What This Means |
|-------|:--------------------------------:|-----------------|
| District Qualifier Winner | 1.0× (baseline) | Beat most teams at a local event |
| District Qualifier Playoff Winner | 1.2-1.3× | Best alliance at a local event |
| District Championship Winner | 1.4-1.6× | Top ~30 teams in your district |
| Worlds Division Winner | 1.6-1.9× | Top ~50 teams in the world per division |
| Einstein Winner | 1.9-2.3× | Best alliance in the world |

**Einstein winners score roughly DOUBLE what district qualifier winners score.** The gap is that large.

### Where the score jumps come from

| Score Component | District Qual → Einstein | Growth |
|----------------|:------------------------:|:------:|
| Auto scoring | 1-2 pieces → 4-5 pieces | 2-3× |
| Teleop cycle speed | 6-7s cycles → 3.5-4s cycles | 1.5-2× |
| Teleop accuracy | 70% scored → 95% scored | 1.3× |
| Endgame | Attempted → Perfect every match | Reliability |
| Alliance coordination | Independent play → Synchronized strategy | Multiplier |

**The biggest single improvement from district to Einstein is AUTO.** Going from 1-piece auto to 4-piece auto roughly doubles your auto contribution. This is free points with zero driver skill required.

## The Math: What Cycles Per Match Gets You Where

Assuming 135-second teleop, with overhead for driving between
scoring positions:

| Cycle Time | Cycles per Match | Typical Alliance Score | Level |
|:----------:|:----------------:|:---------------------:|-------|
| 8s | ~16 | ~60-80 | District qualifier |
| 6s | ~22 | ~90-110 | District competitive |
| 5s | ~27 | ~115-140 | District championship |
| 4.5s | ~30 | ~135-165 | Worlds divisional |
| 4s | ~33 | ~155-190 | Einstein competitive |
| 3.5s | ~38 | ~180-210+ | Einstein winning |

**Every 0.5 seconds saved per cycle ≈ 3-5 extra scoring actions per match ≈ 10-20 extra points.**

This is why R11 (Cycle Speed is King) has 100% accuracy across
14 games. The math is undefeated.
